# Case Study: Rendetalje Worker

## Summary

Rendetalje Worker is an operations platform concept for a cleaning business.

The goal is to support real business operations such as leads, bookings, cleaning tasks, customer communication, planning, and internal overview.

---

## Problem

A cleaning business has many operational moving parts:

- Incoming leads
- Customer requests
- Calendar planning
- Employee task lists
- One-time jobs
- Recurring jobs
- Follow-ups
- Invoicing preparation
- Operational changes

If this is managed manually, it becomes easy to miss details or lose overview.

---

## Idea

Rendetalje Worker explores how a business-specific internal tool can support daily operations.

The system should help turn operational information into clear actions.

Examples:

- Which customers need follow-up?
- What jobs are planned this week?
- Which leads are most relevant?
- What should be sent to the customer?
- What tasks should employees receive?

## What I learned

This project taught me that business automation has to fit the real workflow.

A useful system must be simple enough for daily use, structured enough to reduce mistakes, and flexible enough to handle changes.
