# Case Study: Rendetalje Workflows

## Summary

Rendetalje Workflows is a practical AI-assisted operations project connected to a real cleaning business.

The goal is to use structured workflows, customer information, calendar data, and AI-assisted writing to reduce manual admin work and improve follow-up.

---

## Problem

Cleaning operations involve many small but important tasks:

- New leads
- Customer follow-up
- Calendar planning
- Weekly task lists
- Employee coordination
- Customer communication
- Quotes and reminders
- Invoice preparation

When these tasks are handled manually across email, calendar, notes, and messages, it becomes easy to miss opportunities or lose overview.

---

## Idea

The idea is to build practical workflows that help turn business information into concrete actions.

Examples:

- Convert new leads into follow-up messages
- Identify which leads are worth contacting
- Turn calendar events into weekly task lists
- Prepare customer communication
- Support operational planning
- Keep better overview of active and inactive customers

## Tools and technologies

- AI prompts and LLM-assisted workflows
- Structured lead data
- Gmail workflow concepts
- Google Calendar workflow concepts
- Business process rules
- Customer communication templates
- Operational planning logic

---

## What I learned

This project taught me that automation must stay close to the real business process.

The best workflows are simple, understandable, and controlled. AI is most useful when it helps prepare decisions, draft communication, and organize information — while a human still reviews important actions.
