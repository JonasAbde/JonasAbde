# Case Study: Foodtruck Fiesta

## Summary

Foodtruck Fiesta is a business platform and web project connected to food truck events, customer communication, service presentation, and booking workflows.

The project explores how a local service business can use digital tools to present services, manage interest, and support event-based operations.

---

## Problem

Event-based businesses need to handle many different customer needs:

- Event inquiries
- Service explanation
- Booking requests
- Customer communication
- Availability questions
- Pricing and offer preparation
- Web presence

If the customer journey is unclear, potential customers may not understand the offer or know how to take the next step.

---

## Idea

The idea is to create a clearer digital experience around the business.

The platform should help with:

- Presenting the service clearly
- Supporting customer inquiries
- Structuring booking interest
- Improving trust and professionalism
- Making follow-up easier

## What I learned

This project taught me that business websites should not only look good. They should guide the customer toward a clear action.

For service businesses, the most important parts are clarity, trust, speed, and easy communication.
