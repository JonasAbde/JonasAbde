# Labs

This page lists the experiment areas I am interested in.

## AI workflow labs

Experiments around turning messy information into structured actions.

Examples:

- Lead follow-up assistant with sample data
- Email-to-task extraction
- Calendar-to-weekly-plan workflow
- Customer communication drafting
- Internal decision-support prompts

## Agent labs

Experiments around AI agents and tool-based workflows.

Examples:

- Agent workspace patterns
- Prompt libraries
- Multi-step task execution
- Human approval checkpoints
- Tool permission design

## Business automation labs

Experiments around small-business operations.

Examples:

- Cleaning business operations
- Event and booking workflows
- Customer segmentation
- Follow-up sequencing
- Internal admin support

## Product prototype labs

Experiments around turning ideas into product concepts.

Examples:

- AI dashboards
- Assistant UIs
- Internal tools
- Workflow operating systems
- Simulation systems

## Infrastructure labs

Experiments around AI runtime and technical systems.

Examples:

- Self-hosted AI concepts
- API architecture
- Model serving ideas
- Docker-based workflows
- Local development systems

## Lab principle

A lab project does not need to be perfect. It should teach something concrete and move a real idea forward.
